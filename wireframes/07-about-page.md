## Component States Summary

### Cards
- **Default:** White/gray background, subtle shadow
- **Hover:** Shadow increase, slight lift
- **Loading:** Skeleton loader

### Buttons
- **Default:** Primary/secondary styling
- **Hover:** Scale 1.05, shadow increase
- **Loading:** Spinner, disabled

---

## Copy Guidelines

- **Tone:** Authentic, inspiring, trustworthy
- **Story:** Personal, narrative-driven
- **Values:** Clear, benefit-focused
- **Team:** Human, approachable
- **CTAs:** Action-oriented, benefit-focused

---

## Visual Elements

- **Images:** High-quality, authentic photos
- **Icons:** Consistent style, meaningful
- **Colors:** Brand colors (forest green, stone gray, sky blue)
- **Typography:** Bold headlines, readable body text
- **Spacing:** Generous whitespace

- **Meta Title:** "About Beyond Peaks - Our Story, Mission & Team"
- **Meta Description:** Compelling summary of company
- **Structured Data:** Organization schema
- **Internal Links:** Link to treks, contact, stories
